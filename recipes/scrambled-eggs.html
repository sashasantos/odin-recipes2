Fluffy Scrambled Eggs

Follow these simple steps to make the absolute best scrambled eggs- pillow-y, fluffy, creamy, and flavorful eggs every time!

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat with whisk.
  3. Add milk to eggs and season to taste.
  4. Heat and add butter to pan.
  5. Pour in eggs and let cook for up to a minute.
  6. Gently stir and turn eggs until all uncooked parts become firm.
  7. Transfer to plate, serve, and enjoy.
